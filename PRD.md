# PRD
## Description
OkTolk is a mobile Progressive Web App (PWA) assistant designed for users aged 40+ and elderly. It helps protect against scams, simplifies news consumption, and provides easy voice-based interaction. The MVP focuses on core safety and communication features, assuming users have a smartphone with internet access, a Telegram account for alerts, and are comfortable with basic voice commands.
## Features
- AI chatbot (text/photo analysis for scam detection; answers everyday questions)
- Suspicious message/call risk level (0-3) reporting
- News feed with automatic simplification and translation into plain language
- Voice input (STT) and news read-aloud (TTS)
- Telegram notifications to trusted contacts when a scam is detected
## Success Criteria
- Chatbot can analyze a text or photo for scam indicators and respond within 2 seconds
- Risk level is correctly assigned (0-3) for at least 90% of confirmed scam examples
- News articles are reduced to key points in simple language with >80% user satisfaction
- Voice input works with >95% accuracy for common elderly speech patterns
- Trusted contacts receive Telegram alerts within 10 seconds of scam detection
